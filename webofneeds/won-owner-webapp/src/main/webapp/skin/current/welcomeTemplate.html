Welcome to Matchat!

How many opportunities in life are lost forever because the people involved did not notice them? We're here to help!

On Matchat, you post your atoms, wishes, interests, offers - one by one. When they are matched with others, you can chat with them and see if it's one of these opportunities.

To get started, most users post about what they would like to do in their free time, some prefer to advertise their professional services, others post about their shopping atoms.

Up to you!
