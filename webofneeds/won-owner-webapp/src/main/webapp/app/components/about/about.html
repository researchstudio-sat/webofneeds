What is the Web of Needs?
What is a «atom»? An atom helps you find people who can help you - or who share your interest. Read more An atom is much like an automatic classified ad. You say what you are looking for, and other such ads will be matched with yours. You could think of it as of a long-lived search query that can itself be found by others. Once you found a useful match, you can connect to it and start to chat.
